Personal Information

When you leave your email address you access the Site, pursuant to ProsVest’s Terms of Use. Except as required by law, ProsVest agrees to maintain your non-public information in strict confidence. When you use the Site, ProsVest’s servers automatically record certain information about your use of the Site. Similar to other web services, ProsVest records information such as browsing activity, data displayed or clicked on (such as UI elements, ads, and links), and other information (such as browser type, IP address, date and time of access, cookie ID, and referrer URL). Along with cookies, ProsVest may also use third-party tracking technology, such as Google Analytics, to record similar information regarding you and your activity on the Site. ProsVest is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child under the age of 18 has provided us with personally identifiable information without his or her consent, he or she should contact us at prosvest@gmail.com. ProsVest will delete such information as soon as we become aware of it.

Use of Information

ProsVest maintains and processes your ProsVest account and its contents to provide you with periodic product updates and non-specific information. ProsVest may send you information related to the ProsVest blog. If you decide at any time that you no longer wish to receive communications from us, please follow the “unsubscribe” instructions provided in the communications or email us at prosvest@gmail.com

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties except as permitted or required by law or if we believe there has been a violation of the ProsVest Terms of Use. You understand and agree that any personal information that you provide through the Site and explicitly agree to share publicly might be made publicly available in ProsVest’s discretion, and that other Users may use and reproduce such. We have no control over the use by other Users of the information that you voluntarily disclose to ProsVest for this purpose or to the public in general. This includes comments on blog posts or any other information related to common content contributions on the Site.

Removing Yourself from ProsVest

You may request deletion of personal information by emailing prosvest@gmail.com and providing us enough information to identify your account and prove that you are the owner of the identified account. We will endeavor to act on these deletion requests as soon as reasonably practicable. You may decline to provide personal information to ProsVest.
